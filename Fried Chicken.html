Fried Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1kg chicken pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in buttermilk for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  2. Mix flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Heat oil in a deep skillet or fryer to 175°C (350°F).
  4. Remove chicken from buttermilk, dredge in flour mixture, and shake off excess.
  5. Fry the chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes per batch.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
